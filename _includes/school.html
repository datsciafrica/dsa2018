Since 2015, Data Science Africa (DSA) has held an annual summer school and workshop to train participants on machine learning and data science methods and provide an avenue for researchers to present work demonstrating the application of these techniques to problems relevant in the African context.

For 2018, there will be two events: DSA 2018 Nyeri in Kenya and DSA 2018 Abuja in Nigeria representing our goal to consolidate the regional community in East Africa while also expanding to other regions. DSA 2018 Nyeri will focus on training approximately 30 participants who will then be able to serve as trainers in their local contexts. DSA 2018 Nyeri will be a "Training of Trainers" event.

We would like to expose data science trainers to the entire pipeline of data collection, analysis, and communication of results using relevant examples. We plan to have participants deploy sensor systems at the DeKUT conservancy and farm which will collect data that they will then analyse. DSA 2018 Nyeri will be organised under the theme "end-to-end data science."

To benefit from this course participants must have some background in programming particularly programming with Python and machine learning. The registration process will include submission of worked examples in Jupyter notebooks.
