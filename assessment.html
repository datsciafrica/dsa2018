Pre-requisite Jupyter Notebooks

Data Science Africa - 2018 Nyeri is targetted at equipping Trainers of Trainers in data science focusing on an end-to-end presentation of the content. The summer school is thus longer than the usual one so that material is covered in greater depth with room for sufficient exercises.

To this end, we require that participants are well versed with the basics of the technologies and languages that will be used in the summer school. Particularly we want to make sure participants have sufficient base skills in Python programming, Data science and Machine learning. You are required to download the corresponding notebooks under the different headings to right (by clicking them ofcourse) complete the notebooks and then fill out the registration form below that requires you to upload the completed notebooks. Attendance to the workshop and possible travel grants will be based on your performance in these notebooks.

Good luck ! If somethings in the notebooks are unclear, Google is your friend. Some possible reference material can also be found here: Learn python, the all round reference Data camp.
